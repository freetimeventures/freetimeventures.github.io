ABOUT US

Disruptive Innovations For A Connected Future

FreeTime Ventures is a mobile app development studio founded in 2013. We build apps that make people more productive in their lives, whether it's helping them manage their time, get more out of their shopping experiences, or stay organized on the go.

Our team has a passion for building beautiful and user-friendly apps that solve real problems for real people. We're constantly experimenting with new ideas and technologies to push the boundaries of what's possible on mobile devices.

We're headquartered in New York City, but our team members are located all over the world. We're a close-knit group of passionate individuals who are constantly learning and growing together.
